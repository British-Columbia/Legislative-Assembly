#### Short title

1. This Act may be cited as the Constitution (Amendment) Act.

#### Amendment

2. RSBC 2024, c. 1 is amended as set out in the Schedule.

3. Sections 9, 10 and 11 of RSBC 2024, c. 19 are struck.
